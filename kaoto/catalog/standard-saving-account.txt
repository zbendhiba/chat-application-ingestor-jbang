With the "Elite Money Market Account" saving account earn 4.50% APY on balances of $20,000 or more. It offers tiered interest rates and great benefits.

The account is available for personal and business customers, but is limited to one account per customer.
**Fees and Requirements:**
* The monthly maintenance fee is $10.00 if the balance falls below $2,500.00.
* The minimum opening deposit is $100.

With the "Elite Money Market Account", you will enjoy all the benefits of a traditional checking account, but with tiered interest rates that pay more for higher balances than a standard savings account. Plus you can access your funds at any time unlike with certificates of deposit that require your money tyo be untouched for a set timeframe.

**Additional Benefits:**
* Free mobile banking app with bill pay and account management
* Online investment services, such as IRAs or annuities, through a partnership with a third-party provider
* Tiered interest rates for linked savings accounts or CDs

**Transaction Details:**
* Unlimited check writing with no fees
* Free ATM transactions worldwide
